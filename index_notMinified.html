About

Geef Capital is a Family Office firm that was founded in 2004. The firm manages five distinct long/short equity strategies from its offices in Tel Aviv and New York and is Israel’s first and largest hedge fund manager. The firm’s investment philosophy is based on a disciplined and repeatable process that is focused on fundamental investing and is overseen by experienced investment teams, currently consisting of over twenty professionals. Sphera’s investment approach is dedicated to pursuing strong, consistent risk-adjusted returns across distinct market cycles and macro events. The investment team is supported by a robust business and operational infrastructure that is led by experienced professionals. The firm has a diverse investor base that includes long-standing relationships with some of Israel’s largest and most prominent pensions, insurance companies, financial institutions and family offices.

Sphera strategy

Founded in 2004, Sphera’s strategy aims to generate returns that exceed the relevant indices on a risk-adjusted basis. Utilizing a long/short equity approach to investing, the strategy focuses on securities of large and mid-cap Israeli and Israeli-related companies mainly on the Tel Aviv 125. Over the years, Sphera has built a unique team of analysts with long-standing experience who seek to identify and access the most attractive investment opportunities.

Small Cap

A long/short equity strategy, Sphera Small-Cap was founded in October 2011 and focuses on investments in equities of companies traded on the second and third-tier indices on the Tel Aviv Stock Exchange. These companies are often undervalued due to the absence of extensive analytical coverage or in light of lower tradability compared to the leading indices. The long-standing experience of the investment team seeks to provide investors with an edge in identifying and accessing attractive investment opportunities in this particular realm.

Global Healthcare

With a focus on investments in the global pharmaceutical and biotechnology sectors, this long/short equity strategy was founded in 2007. Sphera Global Healthcare’s investments are based on comprehensive scientific research conducted by a team of scientists, doctors, and investment experts operating in New York and Tel Aviv. The team of analysts is organized by therapeutic category, allowing deep insights and expertise in treatment modalities.

Biotech

Founded in 2018, Sphera Biotech focuses on highly innovative small and mid-cap companies in the global biotechnology sector. Sphera Biotech’s investments are based on comprehensive scientific research conducted by teams of scientists, doctors, and investment experts operating in New York and Tel Aviv. The team of analysts is organized by therapeutic category, allowing deep insights and expertise into treatment modalities. The investment team’s research process seeks to use this enhanced perspective to detect disruptive technologies at an early stage.
